Electronic Devices

If you manufacture, import or sell electronic products on a European market, this must mean that you must strictly comply with the European WEEE Directive, which states the treating of waste electrical and electronic equipment, to contribute to sustainable production and consumption.

Battery Disposal

If you manage a company that manufactures, imports or sell batteries or if you sell electronic products that involve the use of batteries in Europe, this must mean that you must strictly comply with the European Directive on Batteries (Directive 2006/66/EC), commonly known as 'Battery Directive', which states that the manufacture and disposal of batteries in the European Union must be done in a proper, precise way in the aims of improving the environmental performance of batteries and accumulators.

Enquire with us now and we will take care of the rest!

Document Shredding

The protection of your documents is our highest priority, and we guarantee you that. We are also fully aware of the responsibilities of managing documents containing confidential data of your organisation and your client hence why we supply our clients with lockable shredding consoles with security tags to ensure their safety. All data is destroyed/shredded within 24 hours of collection, strictly following BSIA European standards before being transferred directly to contracted paper mills and recycled as raw materials to generate paper-based products.
